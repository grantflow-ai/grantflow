# Melanoma Alliance Research Grant

## Call for Proposals 2025

### Background and Significance

The Melanoma Alliance is committed to funding innovative research that advances our understanding of melanoma biology and develops new therapeutic approaches. This call for proposals seeks projects that address critical gaps in melanoma research and have the potential to improve patient outcomes.

### Research Areas of Interest

- Novel therapeutic targets and biomarkers
- Immunotherapy development and optimization
- Drug resistance mechanisms
- Early detection and diagnostic tools
- Prevention strategies

### Proposal Requirements

#### Research Plan (Maximum 10 pages)
Provide a comprehensive description of the proposed research including:

- **Specific Aims**: Clear, achievable objectives with measurable outcomes
- **Background and Significance**: Rationale and importance of the research
- **Preliminary Data**: Evidence supporting the feasibility of the project
- **Research Design and Methods**: Detailed experimental approach
- **Expected Outcomes**: Potential impact on melanoma research and patient care

#### Budget and Timeline
- Total budget not to exceed $500,000 over 3 years
- Detailed budget justification required
- Clear timeline with major milestones

#### Research Team
- Principal Investigator qualifications and experience
- Key personnel and their roles
- Institutional support and resources

### Evaluation Criteria

Proposals will be evaluated based on:
- Scientific merit and innovation (40%)
- Feasibility and approach (30%)
- Potential impact (20%)
- Investigator qualifications (10%)

### Application Deadline
Applications must be submitted by March 15, 2025.
