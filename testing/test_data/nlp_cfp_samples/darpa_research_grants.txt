European Research Council Starting Grant 2025
Call for Proposals

HORIZON-ERC-2025-STG

1. Objectives
The ERC Starting Grant (STG) supports excellent Principal Investigators who are at the career stage at which they are starting their own independent research team or programme. The scheme targets promising researchers who have the proven potential to be research leaders.

2. Funding
Up to EUR 1.5 million for up to 5 years
Additional EUR 1 million available for equipment, large instruments, access to large facilities, computing resources, and other major experimental and field work costs.

3. Principal Investigator Eligibility
The Principal Investigator must:
• Be of any nationality
• Have been awarded their first PhD at least 2 and at most 7 years prior to the call deadline
• Demonstrate scientific independence
• Show evidence of research maturity and potential for research leadership

Career breaks and other circumstances affecting research careers will be taken into account according to the ERC guidelines.

4. Host Institution Requirements
• Must be established in an EU Member State or Associated Country
• Must provide written confirmation of hosting arrangements
• Must guarantee scientific independence to the PI
• Must have a Gender Equality Plan in place (for public bodies, research organizations, and higher education institutions)

5. Proposal Structure
Applications must not exceed the following page limits:

Part A: Administrative forms (no page limit)

Part B1: Extended Synopsis (5 pages maximum)
- Summary of proposed research
- Principal Investigator information
- Early achievements track record

Part B2: Scientific Proposal (15 pages maximum including references)
Section a: State-of-the-art and objectives (4 pages)
Section b: Methodology (6 pages) 
Section c: Resources and project management (3 pages)
Section d: Ethical and safety issues (2 pages)

6. Evaluation Criteria
Proposals are evaluated solely on the criterion of scientific excellence, assessed through:

Ground-breaking nature and potential impact (50%)
- Novelty and ambition of the research
- Potential to advance the state-of-the-art
- Interdisciplinary potential

Methodology (30%)
- Appropriateness and feasibility of methodology
- Quality of objectives and research approach
- Handling of risks and contingency plans

Principal Investigator (20%)
- Intellectual capacity and creativity
- Commitment and motivation
- Scientific independence

7. Submission Deadlines
The call has one submission deadline:
4 October 2025, 17:00:00 Brussels local time

Late applications will not be accepted under any circumstances.

8. Application Process
Step 1: Submit Part A (administrative information)
Step 2: Submit Part B1 (Extended Synopsis) - evaluated first
Step 3: Submit Part B2 (Scientific Proposal) - only if invited after Step 2

All submissions must be made through the EU Funding & Tenders Portal.

9. Evaluation Process
Two-step evaluation:
- Step 1: Remote evaluation of Part B1 by expert panels
- Step 2: Remote evaluation and interviews for Part B2 (shortlisted candidates only)

Results expected by July 2026.

10. Restrictions
• Maximum one application per PI to ERC-2025-STG
• PIs cannot hold other ERC grants simultaneously
• No resubmission of identical or substantially similar proposals within the same call
• Proposals must comply with EU research integrity standards

11. Ethics Requirements
All research activities must comply with:
• National legislation and international conventions
• EU Charter of Fundamental Rights
• Ethical principles of the Helsinki Declaration
• Good research practice standards

Special attention required for:
• Research involving human participants
• Research involving animals
• Research involving genetic engineering
• Research in developing countries

12. Open Science Practices
Grant holders are expected to:
• Ensure open access to peer-reviewed publications
• Develop data management plans
• Consider open access to research data when appropriate
• Follow responsible research and innovation principles

For detailed guidance, consult the ERC Work Programme 2025 and Application Guidelines available at: https://erc.europa.eu/funding/starting-grants
